# Content Strategy Rules

## Topic Scope

- Focus exclusively on Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) for medical practices
- Cover the full revenue cycle: front-end (scheduling, eligibility, PA), mid-cycle (documentation, coding, charge capture), and back-end (billing, denials, collections)
- Target audience is practice administrators, billing managers, and RCM leaders at physician practices

## Source Requirements

Aggregate information from these public domain sources every issue:

- **Government:** CMS.gov (Fee Schedules, OPPS, regulatory rules), HHS.gov, Federal Register
- **Professional associations:** AAPC, HFMA, AHA, AHIP, AMA
- **Payer portals:** UnitedHealthcare, Cigna, Aetna, Humana, Blue Cross Blue Shield policy updates
- **Industry publications:** Becker's Healthcare, RevCycleIntelligence, Healthcare IT News, Healthcare Dive, Modern Healthcare, MedCity News
- **Market research:** GlobeNewswire, KLAS Research reports
- **Vendor publications:** Experian Health, Waystar, AKASA, CodaMetrix, Athelas, Aspirion, Quadax, and other notable RCM vendors

Every section must include cited sources with hyperlinks.

## Three Emphasis Pillars

These themes must thread through every issue -- not confined to a single section:

1. **Workflow Automations** ŌĆö Front-end eligibility verification, PA automation, claim scrubbing, payment posting, denial follow-up automation, daily statement cycles
2. **AI Use and Adoption** ŌĆö Autonomous coding, ambient documentation, AI denial management, agentic AI workflows, generative AI for appeals, predictive analytics
3. **Technology to Improve Revenue Velocity and Efficiency** ŌĆö RPA, clean claims rate optimization, Days in A/R reduction, KPI dashboards, EHR/PM system integrations, FHIR APIs

## Content Structure (10 Sections Per Issue)

1. **Regulatory/Payment Updates** ŌĆö CMS rules, fee schedule changes, payer policy shifts
2. **Prior Authorization / Payer Relations** ŌĆö PA reform, payer commitments, denial trends
3. **AI & Automation Deep Dive** ŌĆö Latest AI tools, adoption stats, case studies, ROI data
4. **Coding Updates** ŌĆö CPT, ICD-10, HCPCS changes relevant to the current cycle
5. **Revenue Velocity / KPIs** ŌĆö Benchmarks, strategies, operational best practices
6. **Technology Spotlight** ŌĆö Vendor news, KLAS rankings, tech stack evolution, RPA market
7. **Compliance Corner** ŌĆö Price transparency, interoperability, audit prep, regulatory deadlines
8. **Independent Practice Watch** Ś RCM challenges and opportunities specific to independent (non-hospital-owned) physician practices. Covers how the week's regulatory, payer, and technology changes affect solo and small-group practices differently from large health systems. Includes a "Where You Should Be" benchmark showing where independent practices stand vs. industry targets and what to prioritize next.
9. **Specialty RCM Spotlight** Ś Quick-hit updates for major specialties covering billing changes, coding updates, payer policy shifts, or reimbursement trends relevant that week. Always include at minimum: Primary Care, Cardiology, Orthopedics, and Oncology Ś add others (Radiology, Neurology, Mental Health, Gastroenterology, etc.) when relevant news warrants. Format as a scannable table or short labeled subsections per specialty, each with at least one data point or update.
10. **This Week's Action Items** ŌĆö Specific, actionable checklist tied to the issue's content

Each section must include:
- A section number label (e.g., "Section 01")
- A compelling headline
- At least one data point or statistic
- A callout box (insight, warning, or action) where appropriate
- Cited sources at the bottom

## Closing Element

Every issue ends with a "Big Stat" ŌĆö a single, impactful data point presented as a visual hero block that reinforces the urgency of RCM optimization.
